Welcome to the Bridgeans Family

The Windsor Bridgeans Organization (WBO) started in 2011 by two (2) brothers. Windsor Bridgeans Organization is a non profit organization, a filipino sports group that focuses on basketball and other sports activities in the Windsor and Essex County. The name was established by Mr. Gil Panganiban, founder of The North American Basketball Association (NABA) and an avid advisor of WBO.

We are also dedicated to greater diversity within the organization and provide a fun, safe, healthy, respectful, rewarding, and equal sports experience to people of all culture, genders, age, abilities and status in the community.

We take pride in developing a genuine sense of loyalty and aspire to help our youth and adults achieve high standards of fair play and good sportsmanship. At the same time, we like to maintain good culture by volunteering our services throughout the community and donate when we can.
